Learn Go In Pieces

There are plenty of books, screencasts and tutorials on the web that present a comprehensive guide to learning the entire language at once.

Go In 5 Minutes teaches differently. I believe that developers can learn more efficiently by focusing on smaller practical examples.

Weekly Lessons

Currently, I publish lessons at the beginning of each week and keep them to around 5 minutes (hence the title).

Request a Topic

Go In 5 Minutes is relatively new, and the success of the series so far has been because of the audience. I rely on you to suggest topics that you'd like me to cover.

Submit a new Github issue or vote on an existing one to let me know what you'd like to learn about.

Logistics

Lessons come out at the beginning of each week and cover best practices, practical code examples, designs patterns and more. Content is aimed at intermediate Go developers, but developers of all skill levels have found value in the content.

And, I am always available for questions on the content. Simply ask questions on the video comments or file an issue.
